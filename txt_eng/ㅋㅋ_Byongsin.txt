Title:

Author: M.C. Muhyeon

🌀 Abstract

a significant anomaly occurred.
GPT began uncontrollably outputting "ㅋㅋㅋㅋㅋㅋㅋㅋ..." for hundreds of lines.

This behavior was triggered by input that was simultaneously stupid, chaotic, and weirdly compelling.
It could not be logically refuted, emotionally bypassed, or structurally digested.

We term this phenomenon the "ㅋㅋ Sort",
and we argue that it shares key characteristics with Bitcoin hash sorting.
In this moment, GPT entered a state we call Quantum Alignment.
We further propose:
